What is a cryptocurrency?

A Crypto currency, like Bitcoin, is a virtual currency that is not controlled by a central goverment or body. Instead is has many "nodes" which keeps the ledger, or transaction record, correct and up to date. The "miners" do this by utilzing specialized computeres to solve math problems, or hashs, that make sure the currency is not being forged. For more information take a look at this quick youtube video explaining the basics of Bitcoin.
